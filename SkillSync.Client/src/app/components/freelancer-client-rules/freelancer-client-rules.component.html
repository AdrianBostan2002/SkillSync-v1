Freelancer Obligations:
Accurate Project Details:
  • Provide accurate and comprehensive details about the project, including scope, requirements, and deliverables.
  • Ensure that project descriptions are clear, concise, and free of misleading information.
Timely Communication:
  • Respond promptly to client inquiries, messages, and project updates.
  • Maintain open and transparent communication throughout the project lifecycle.
Quality Workmanship:
  • Deliver high-quality work that meets or exceeds client expectations.
  • Adhere to project deadlines and milestones as agreed upon with the client.
Professional Conduct:
  • Conduct oneself in a professional manner at all times, both in communication and behavior.
  • Respect client preferences, feedback, and confidentiality requirements.
Compliance with Platform Policies:
    Abide by the terms of service, community guidelines, and any other platform policies. Refrain from engaging in any prohibited activities or behavior that violates platform rules.
Client Obligations: Clear Project Requirements:
  • Provide detailed and clear project requirements, objectives, and specifications.
  • Communicate any changes or updates to project parameters in a timely manner.
Timely Feedback:
  • Provide timely feedback and direction to freelancers to ensure project progress and quality.
  • Address any concerns or issues promptly to avoid delays or misunderstandings.
Payment and Compensation:
  • Honor payment agreements and compensate freelancers fairly for their work.
  • Adhere to agreed-upon payment schedules and methods outlined in project contracts.
Respectful Communication:
  • Maintain respectful and professional communication with freelancers throughout the project duration.
  • Provide constructive feedback and guidance to freelancers to facilitate project success.
Compliance with Platform Policies:
  • Abide by the terms of service, user guidelines, and any other platform policies.
  • Report any violations or misconduct observed on the platform to ensure a safe and positive environment for all
    • users.
Including these obligations on your publish page can help set clear expectations for both freelancers and clients, fostering transparency, accountability, and successful project outcomes. Additionally, consider providing links to detailed terms of service or user agreements where users can find more information about their rights and responsibilities on your platform.
